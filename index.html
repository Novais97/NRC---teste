" Custom, code-compliant decks built for Canadian weather.
We design and build safe, durable, and beautiful wood decks to enhance your outdoor living."



What we do during your deck project:

✔ Base & Structure: We dig holes, pour concrete, and install strong posts to hold the deck safely above the ground — this prevents the deck from sinking or shifting in winter.


✔ Wood Frame: We use treated wood for the frame (called joists and beams). It's protected against rot, bugs, and moisture.


✔ Surface Deck Boards: You can choose the wood you like best: – Pressure-treated (strong and affordable) – Cedar (natural look and smell) – Composite (no need to paint or sand) – PVC (great around pools)


✔ Stairs & Railings: We install safe steps with railings, all secured and level.


✔ Finishing: We sand the surface, check every screw, and finish edges for comfort and safety. Optional: apply stain or sealer for protection and color.


✔ You can also add:


  • Privacy screens
  • LED lights
  • Built-in benches
  • Covered areas (like pergolas)
  • BBQ or dining space

    • ✔ Built for Canadian Homes:


  • We follow local building codes (OBC)
  • All decks made to hold heavy snow
  • We can help with city permits if needed
